CHAPTER ONE

WILLIAMSON TUNNELS

In 2015, RAW's journey began. The concept was simple: unused, intimate spaces with a huge sound system and and quality DJ's. The music would always be centric to the experience, offering ravers an opportunity to experience a party outside of the 'big room'. With this in mind, the curators began searching for the perfect venue, and not long after discovered an underground tunnel network deep in the heart of the city. The venue was the Williamson Tunnels, a grade two listed heritage site consisting of a low ceiling, arched tunnelway which trapped in the sound of the VOID soundsystem and created the intensity that RAW was seeking. This became RAW's spiritual home for years after. At the time, attending a RAW party was the only opportunity you could experience this venue. Since then it has gone on to establish itself as one of the most notorious events spaces in the UK.
